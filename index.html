Please look through the videos and mark any that are problematic according to the critera below.

We are putting together a data set of videos that resemble natural home videos. The videos should NOT:

  • contain inappropriate or objectionable content. This includes (but is not limited to): violence, sexuality, nudity, or profanity.
  • contain visible captions (e.g. a newscast), watermarks, or textual overlays (but it is ok to keep clips with timestamps or small logos at the top or bottom edges of the video)
  • be a cartoon, animation, video game clip, or other non-natural video
  • have a vertical orientation, low resolution, or degraded format
  • have abrupt cuts, are sped up or slowed down, or otherwise show signs of significant post-processing

Please flag videos that do not meet these criteria by selecting the button marked "Invalid" and then checking off any violations that you noticed. Note that if you are not sure if something is appropriate, you can mark it as "uncomfortable".

Note: You do NOT have to vet the audio content of the videos, ONLY the visual content.
